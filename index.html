Estria — Realtor Text Assistant

The text assistant for a licensed real-estate agent.

Clients text the agent's published number with questions about listings and showings. The assistant replies on the agent's behalf, schedules visits, and confirms appointments — a normal two-way conversation by text.

How it works

1

You reach out first. You find the agent's number on their website, a listing, a yard sign, or a business card, and you text it with a question.

2

The agent's assistant replies. It answers questions about available listings, helps you find a time to tour, and confirms the details — all in the same text thread.

3

You get the updates you asked for. Showing confirmations and reminders for appointments you scheduled. Nothing promotional.

About these messages

Messages are consumer-initiated — the agent only texts people who texted first or gave the agent their number to be contacted. There is no marketing and no purchased lists. Message frequency varies by your conversation; message and data rates may apply. Reply HELP for help and STOP to opt out at any time.
